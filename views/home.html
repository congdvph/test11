Explore the history of
Atlanta

The city of Atlanta is also known as the Big Peach for its abundance of the juicy stone fruit. Atlanta continues to grow tremendously each year and adds even more things to do! Enjoy free live music every Sunday at Park Tavern in Piedmont Park, absorb the scenery along the Atlanta BeltLine and feast at Ponce City Market. Learn about Martin Luther King and Margaret Mitchell, and tour the Georgia Aquarium and Coca Cola Factory in Downtown Atlanta.
